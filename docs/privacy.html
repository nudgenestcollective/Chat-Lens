Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

1. About This Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Nudge Lens ("the Extension"), developed and operated by Nudge Nest Collective, handles information when you use the Extension. This policy is intended to comply with the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA, Canada), Ontario privacy law, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and applicable United States federal privacy requirements.

By installing or using the Extension, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

2. What the Extension Accesses

When you use Nudge Lens on ChatGPT (chatgpt.com) or Google Gemini (gemini.google.com), the Extension reads the text content of AI-generated responses displayed on those pages. This reading is performed locally within your browser for the sole purpose of computing an epistemic risk score. This text is never copied, stored, or transmitted anywhere.

3. How Your Data Is Handled

All processing is local. Nudge Lens operates entirely within your browser — no data ever leaves your device.

The text content of AI responses is:

  • Read from the page you are currently viewing
  • Analyzed locally using pattern-matching algorithms built into the Extension
  • Never transmitted to any external server, API, or third party
  • Never stored beyond the current browser session

4. Extension Settings

Nudge Lens stores two user-preference settings using Chrome's built-in chrome.storage.sync:

  • Whether the Extension is enabled (on/off)
  • Sensitivity level (low / medium / high)

These settings contain no personal information. They are synced across your Chrome profile using Google's Chrome sync infrastructure, subject to Google's Privacy Policy. We do not access, read, or transmit these settings.
