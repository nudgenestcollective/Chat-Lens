🔍 Chat Lens

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 8, 2026

Overview

Chat Lens is a Chrome browser extension that analyzes AI-generated responses for epistemic risk. This Privacy Policy explains what data the extension accesses and how it is handled.

Data We Access

When you use Chat Lens on ChatGPT (chatgpt.com) or Google Gemini (gemini.google.com), the extension reads the text content of AI-generated responses displayed on those pages for the sole purpose of computing an epistemic risk score.

How Your Data Is Handled

All processing is local. Chat Lens operates entirely within your browser — no data ever leaves your device.

The text content of AI responses is:

  • Read from the page you are currently viewing
  • Analyzed locally using pattern-matching algorithms built into the extension
  • Never transmitted to any external server, API, or third party
  • Never stored beyond the current browser session

Extension Settings

Chat Lens stores two settings using Chrome's built-in chrome.storage.sync:

  • Whether the extension is enabled (on/off)
  • Sensitivity level (low / medium / high)

These settings contain no personal information and are synced across your Chrome profile using Google's Chrome sync infrastructure, subject to Google's own privacy policy.

Data We Do Not Collect

Personal information
Browsing history
Chat conversation content
Cookies or trackers
Location data
Any data leaving your device

Third-Party Services

Chat Lens does not integrate with or transmit data to any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

Chat Lens does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.
