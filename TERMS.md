## 2. Description of Service

Chat Lens is a browser extension that analyzes AI-generated responses for epistemic risk signals — such as overconfident language, lack of cited evidence, and domain-specific risk (medical, legal, financial). It displays a traffic-light rating (🟢/🟡/🔴) intended to help users think critically about AI output.
