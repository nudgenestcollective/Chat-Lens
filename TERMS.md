Chat Lens is a free browser extension that analyzes AI-generated responses for epistemic risk signals — such as overconfident language, lack of cited evidence, and domain-specific risk (medical, legal, financial, relationship). It displays a traffic-light rating (🟢/🟡/🔴) intended to help users think critically about AI output.
